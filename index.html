ODYSSEY 2.0

Transform Your Commute into interactive journeys

  • About

    What is Odyssey?

    Odyssey 2.0 is a live, interactive city map that brings visibility to spontaneous urban moments, events, and activities that typically go unnoticed. Unlike traditional navigation apps, which focus on permanent locations, Odyssey 2.0 allows users to share and engage with real-time city happenings—creating an interactive urban experience.

    • A real-time map that highlights spontaneous city activities and moments
    • Allows users to add and explore live events that aren't found on traditional maps
    • Enables community engagement and interaction, fostering a more connected urban experience
    • Captures fleeting but meaningful moments like street performances, temporary art, pop-up markets, and unique weather events

    Why Odyssey?

    Cities are constantly changing, full of small but meaningful experiences that make urban life vibrant. However, these moments often go unnoticed or are only shared in isolated social media posts. Traditional navigation tools don't support the discovery of such real-time events, and social platforms don't encourage interaction with the city itself. Odyssey 2.0 bridges this gap, creating a shared, interactive urban space where people can find and contribute to the evolving life of the city.

    Why It Matters:

    • Captures real-time city moments that are otherwise ephemeral
    • Encourages city engagement, making urban spaces more interactive
    • Goes beyond static locations, unlike traditional navigation apps
    • Fosters community connections through shared city experiences
    • Enhances urban exploration, making everyday commutes more engaging

    Who is it for?

    This platform is designed for anyone who loves exploring cities in a dynamic, immersive way. It provides a space where people can both share and discover hidden gems in real time.

    Target Audience:

    • Urban Explorers – People who enjoy discovering new places and experiences
    • Daily Commuters – Those looking to make their routine routes more engaging
    • Tourists – Visitors who want a more authentic and dynamic city experience
    • Local Creatives – Street artists, musicians, performers, and event organizers who want to share their work
    • Community Builders – People interested in fostering local engagement and social interaction

    Where does it work?

    Odyssey 2.0 is designed for urban environments where spontaneous interactions define the city experience. It thrives in places that have a rich mix of culture, community, and dynamic public spaces.

    Ideal Locations:

    • Metropolitan cities with dense, active public spaces
    • Neighborhoods with street culture—live music, art, and public performances
    • Transit hubs and commutes—subway stations, plazas, and sidewalks
    • Cultural hotspots—historical districts, parks, festivals, and events
    • Anywhere people want to engage with their city in real time
